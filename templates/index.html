About the Content

Ask me anything is a platform that tries its best to provide education to various students across the country on one hand and on the other hand, it also provides a chance for people to work as a tutor and earn points and rewards based on their performance and ratings given by the students. The student and tutors have to simply make their accounts as per their field choices and then students are free to post their doubts regarding various topics that will circulate around tutors. They can post their doubts in the form of text as well as in the form of files as per their wish. The tutors will get a notification as soon as the new question gets posted by the student. He further has an option to either accept the question or to delete or remove it if he does not know the answer or doesn't want to answer. He further has an option to either answer the question at the same time or to save it for later. We have tried our best to help the students as early as possible so for this purpose timing to questions is also among the features of our website whereby the question remains on the website for 24 hours and the tutors have to answer within the time duration. In case of failure, the rating of the tutor gets affected. Also, the students rate the work done by the tutor on a scale of 5 using which the average rating of each tutor is calculated. This is all about the efforts we have done to make your learning as a student and learning as a tutor easier. Do give us a try. We can make sure you won't regret your decision. RegardsTeam Captured.
